TrumpBlockr

Posted by
James Barclay
on 2015-10-26 (Updated: 2020-10-19)

Today I released TrumpBlockr, a WebKit Content Blocker that hides or blocks Donald Trump related content from Mobile Safari.

I had quite a few laughs developing it, and showing it to friends and family. Maybe you’ll get a kick out of it too.

Enjoy it. Or don’t.
